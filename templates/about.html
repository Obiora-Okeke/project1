About Spotify Playlist Generator

Welcome to Playlist Generator, your ultimate destination for discovering new music and creating personalized playlists based on your favorite artists!
Our platform utilizes the Spotify API along with other cutting-edge technologies to enhance your music listening experience and help you explore a world of melodies.

What does Spotify Playlist Generator do?

At Playlist Generator, we understand the frustration of running out of music options by your favorite artist and not knowing which other artists share similar vibes.
Our mission is to solve this problem by generating curated playlists for you, filled with tracks by artists who are similar to your favorite ones.
Whether you're a music enthusiast seeking fresh tunes or an artist searching for inspiration, Playlist Generator is the perfect tool for you.

Features and Functionality:

  1. Discover New Artists:

    Input the name of your favorite artist, and Playlist Generator will use the Spotify API to find similar artists with a similar musical style, genre, or vibe.
    This ensures you always have a steady stream of music that resonates with your preferences.

  2. Personalized Playlists:

    Our platform allows you to create personalized playlists based on the artists you love.
    Easily compile your favorite tracks and explore new ones to keep your playlist fresh and exciting.

  3. User Accounts and Saved Playlists:

    To make your experience even more seamless, we provide user account functionality.
    Create an account and log in to save your favorite playlists. You can access them at any time, from any device.

  4. Lyrics and Insights:

    We have integrated the Genius API to provide you with lyrics and insights into the songs you listen to.
    Dive deeper into the music you love and gain a deeper understanding of the artists' work.

  5. MP3 Downloads from YouTube

    Explore the option to download MP3 versions of your favorite songs directly from YouTube.
    Enjoy offline listening whenever and wherever you want.

Technologies and Tools Used:

  • Python:

    Our backend is powered by Python, enabling efficient data processing and API integrations

  • PythonAnywhere:

    We deploy our application on PythonAnywhere, ensuring it's accessible and functional at all times.

  • HTML/CSS:

    The frontend of Playlist Generator is designed using HTML and CSS, providing a sleek and user-friendly interface.

  • SQLite:

    To store user account information and playlist data securely, we utilize SQLite, a lightweight and efficient database management system.

  • Spotify API:

    We leverage the Spotify API to access an extensive library of music and artist data for playlist generation.

  • Genius API:

    The Genius API enhances the user experience by providing song lyrics and insights.

  • Flask:

    Our web application is built on the Flask framework, ensuring smooth navigation and functionality.
